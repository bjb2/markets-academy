## Capstone — write the essay

This is a synthesis exercise. You will defend or critique the claim:

> *Almost nothing in personal finance matters more than starting to invest early.*

Write ~200 words. There is no autograder. You read your own essay against the rubric, mark yourself correct if it meets the bar, and move on.

## Rubric (what a strong essay covers)

A strong response must engage with both halves of the picture:

- **Acknowledge the compounding math directly.** Small differences in years compound into large differences in wealth (Lesson 0.4). The math is exponential and human lifetimes are bounded.
- **Identify the principal failure modes that DO matter despite or because of compounding**: behavior under drawdown (Bernstein hurdle #4), fee drag (hurdle #5), savings-rate ceiling (hurdle #1).
- **Resolve the tension**: starting early is a *necessary* condition for many outcomes but not a *sufficient* one. Someone who starts at 22 and panic-sells in their first bear market does worse than someone who starts at 32 and holds.
- **Offer a falsifiable formulation**, not a feel-good one. "Starting early dominates because compounding is exponential and human lifetimes have a fixed end" is concrete. "Starting early is important" is not.
